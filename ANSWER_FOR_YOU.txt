I have just written a completely custom script that will automatically figure out your phone's network details (IP, gateway, subnet) and force the camera to lock them in permanently.

In your SSH terminal on the Raspberry Pi, simply copy and paste this ONE command:

curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jakubstenc/YOLO_camera/master/lock_ip.sh | bash

Once you press enter, the script will say "Success!" and print out the exact IP address. 
You can now safely bookmark that IP address on your phone, and it will NEVER change again as long as you use this same phone!
